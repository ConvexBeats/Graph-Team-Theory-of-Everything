## Summary
A governance / engineering-leadership layer that sits **above** the individual product teams (e.g. [[graph-team]], [[prebind-team]], [[devx-team]]) and looks after applications at a portfolio level. Holds project oversight, architectural direction, and cross-team coordination for this re-architecture programme. Headed by [[andrea-read]] (Head of Technology Engineering), with [[will-bone]] as Interim Head of Product and [[rory-beattie]] on programme oversight.
